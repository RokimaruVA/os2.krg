How to sign up for classes?
The website features a section with videos explaining how to sign up for classes at Palace of Schoolchildren №2. Additionally, various directions of classes are displayed, and by clicking on them, you can see the full list of our clubs.
Why don't I see the "sign-up" button next to the selected club on the e-daryn website?
When the sign-up button is not displayed on the website, it may be because you are not within the age range for classes in that club, or enrollment in the club is currently closed.
When can we receive the contract for educational services if we have already signed up for classes?
After signing up for a club, the teacher will provide the contract for services within a few classes.
How can I find out the contact number of the teacher?
When selecting clubs on the e-daryn website, the contact number of the teacher is provided along with other information about the classes.
Can I accompany my child to the classroom door?
No. According to modern safety standards, parents are only allowed in the vestibule area (the area near the entrance and cloakroom) without special permission.

Competitions

We offer a variety of contests, competitions, and projects that contribute not only to academic growth but also to personal development. Here you will find engaging and educational events specifically designed to develop the talents and skills of students.

Hairdressing Art

World of Beauty

Level: city-level

Envent regestration: from November 11 to November 25, 2024

Envent date: November 29, 2024

Age of participants: from 8 to 20 years old

The aim of the competition is to create conditions for improving the skill level among specialized courses, studios, and lyceums that teach hairdressing, as well as to attract children and teenagers to the field of "Hairdressing Art." It also aims to enhance creative activity and the artistic and aesthetic quality of work, and to raise the prestige of the profession of "Hairdresser".
